# EduHive

The MOOCs Management System was created with the goal of streamlining and digitizing the college's online course participation management process. To track student enrollments, certificate submissions, and approvals, many schools now use Google Forms, emails, and handwritten Excel sheets. This frequently results in data loss, delays, and a lack of transparency. Through the creation of a centralized, secure, and role-based web portal, faculty members will be able to quickly validate submissions and produce reports, while students will be able to enroll in MOOCs, upload certificates, and track their approval status with ease. The solution avoids human errors and reduces manual workload by automating these repeated administrative operations. It also enhances record accessibility, organization, and accountability.  

**Objective:**
1. A fully functional web-based system for managing MOOC registrations, enrollments, and certificate submissions. 
2. Secure role-based access enabling students and administrators to perform their tasks efficiently. 
3. Automated verification and tracking process, reducing manual effort and errors. 
4. Improved data organization and report generation for better academic monitoring and decision-making.

**Project Approach**
1.	Design & Strategy: We will design a clean, minimalist dashboard for both students and faculty. 
2.	Technology Stack: HTML, CSS, React, MySQL/SQL. 
3.	Implementation & Testing: We will build a notification system that alerts faculty when a new certificate is uploaded and notifies students once their status is        updated.
